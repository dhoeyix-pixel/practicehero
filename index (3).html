// Comms

Questions, answered

Does slowing down change the pitch?

No — speed and pitch are fully independent. Slow a solo to 50% and it stays in the original key. Shift the pitch and the tempo doesn’t move. That’s the core engineering Practice Hero is built on.

What’s actually free?

Pitch shift with fine-tune cents, speed control from 25–400%, basic A/B looping, auto BPM detection, a 4/4 metronome, and drag-and-drop panel customization — free forever, no account. You also get a daily free window of the Pro features to try them on your own material.

What does Pro add?

Speed Ladder training, a Loop Library (save and name your loops), Session Snapshots, practice stats and notes, and odd time signatures and subdivisions on the metronome.

How does the license work?

After checkout you get a license key by email. Paste it into Practice Hero’s settings, hit ACTIVATE, and Pro unlocks instantly. One key works on up to 3 devices.

Does it work on every YouTube video?

Yes — lessons, live shows, backing tracks, that one bootleg with the weird tuning. If YouTube plays it, you can practice with it.

Who builds this?

One independent developer who got tired of scrubbing YouTube timelines mid-practice. Practice Hero is built by a player, for players — support goes straight to a human at practice.heroix@gmail.com.
